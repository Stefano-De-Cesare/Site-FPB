Game F.A.Q.

  • Q: I can't play the game. What do I do?
      • R: Oh boy please don't say this.
  • Q: I can't do nothing other than move around in this game.
      • R: At the moment there's not much you can do in the game but you can open your inventory, equip weapons, hit the blueish statue near the pond and open the dev console.
  • Q: What are the game controls?
      • R: You can press F1 to open the inventory, F8 to open the dev window, click with the left mouse button to walk and click with the right mouse button to attack.
